Panel

Discuss this component on Hackpad

A panel is used to highlight content by indenting it.

Heading level 2

Example paragraph text

<div class="panel-indent [modifier class]">
  <h2>Heading level 2</h2>
  <p>Example paragraph text</p>
</div>

Form

A panel that includes a form has specific rules applied.

Please enter your fullname 
<div class="panel-indent">
  <legend>Please enter your fullname</legend>
  <fieldset class="form-group">
    <label for="firstname">
      Firstname
      <input id="firstname" type="text" class="form-control" />
    </label>
    <label for="surname">
      Surname
      <input id="surname" type="text" class="form-control" />
    </label>
  </fieldset>
</div>

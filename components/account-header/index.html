When to use this component

If your service is part of the personal tax account.

When not to use this component

If your service is not part of the personal tax account. Use the hHeader instead.

How it works

The account header contains:

  • the HMRC cookie banner
  • the GOV.UK header
  • the Acount menu
  • the language selector if your service is avalible in Welsh
